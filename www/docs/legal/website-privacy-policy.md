# Website Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 30, 2026**

## Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Vectara documentation website at docs.vectara.com ("Website"). This policy is separate from and does not cover the Vectara platform or services. For information about how we handle data in our platform, please refer to our main Privacy Policy.

This Website Privacy Policy describes how Vectara, Inc. ("Vectara," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit our documentation website.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our Website:

- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Information**: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, referring URLs
- **Location Information**: General geographic location based on IP address
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser language, screen resolution

### Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when:

- Submitting feedback or contacting us
- Participating in surveys or community features
- Creating an account on the Vectara Console (governed by separate privacy policy)

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information. See our [Cookie Policy](/docs/legal/cookie-policy) for detailed information about:

- What cookies we use
- Why we use them
- How you can control them

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your information based on the following legal grounds:

- **Consent**: For analytics and marketing cookies (you can withdraw consent at any time)
- **Legitimate Interest**: To operate and improve our Website, analyze usage patterns, and provide support
- **Legal Obligation**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

### Specific Uses

- **Website Operation**: To provide, maintain, and secure our Website
- **Analytics**: To understand how visitors use our Website and improve content and functionality
- **User Experience**: To optimize navigation, troubleshoot issues, and enhance user satisfaction
- **Communication**: To respond to inquiries and provide support
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

- **Provider**: Google LLC
- **Purpose**: Website analytics and performance tracking
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Data Collected**: Usage data, device information, anonymized user interactions

- **Provider**: Vectara, Inc. (internal service)
- **Purpose**: Internal analytics for website usage and performance monitoring

- **Data Storage**: United States

### Reo.dev Session Recording

- **Provider**: Reo.dev
- **Purpose**: Session recording and user experience analysis
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://reo.dev/privacy](https://reo.dev/privacy)

- **Opt-Out**: Use our cookie consent manager to disable marketing cookies

### Docusaurus and Hosting

Our Website is built with Docusaurus and may be hosted on services that have their own privacy practices. We select hosting providers with strong data protection standards.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With third-party service providers who assist in operating our Website (e.g., analytics, hosting)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request
- **Business Transfers**: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
- **Protection of Rights**: To protect our rights, property, safety, or that of our users or others

## International Data Transfers

Vectara is based in the United States. Information collected through our Website may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

- **Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)**: As approved by the European Commission
- **Adequacy Decisions**: Relying on jurisdictions deemed to provide adequate data protection

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 2 years (Google Analytics) or 90 days (Vectara Snow Analytics)
- **Session Recordings**: Retained according to Reo.dev's retention policy
- **Cookie Consent Records**: Retained for 1 year
- **Contact Inquiries**: Retained for as long as necessary to respond and for a reasonable period thereafter

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

## Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

### GDPR Rights (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal information
- **Rectification**: Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your information ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction**: Request limitation of processing
- **Portability**: Request transfer of your information to another service
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent for cookie-based processing at any time

### CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

- **Know**: Request information about data collection and sharing practices
- **Access**: Request access to specific pieces of personal information
- **Delete**: Request deletion of personal information
- **Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
- **Non-Discrimination**: Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your rights

### CIPA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Invasion of Privacy Act (CIPA), we:

- Only record sessions after obtaining explicit consent through our cookie banner
- Clearly disclose session recording in our Cookie Policy
- Provide an easy opt-out mechanism through our cookie consent manager

### Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

- **Email**: privacy@vectara.com
- **Subject Line**: "Privacy Rights Request - Website"

We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).

### Cookie Management

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

- Clicking "Manage Cookie Preferences" in the website footer
- Adjusting your browser settings to block or delete cookies
- Visiting our [Cookie Policy](/docs/legal/cookie-policy) for more information

## Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@vectara.com.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will:

- Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- Notify you of material changes through a prominent notice on our Website or by email

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
